Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This policy explains how Geometrisk Risk Partners Pty Ltd (ABN 72 694 292 496), trading as Geometrisk (“we”, “us”, “our”), collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information. We handle personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). By using this website or contacting us, you agree to this policy.

Disclaimer — general information only

The information on this website — including any tools, examples, figures, and general commentary — is provided for general information purposes only. It is not financial, legal, insurance, or other professional advice, and does not take account of your particular objectives, financial situation, or needs.

You should obtain your own professional advice before acting on anything you read here. To the maximum extent permitted by law, Geometrisk does not accept any liability for any loss or damage arising from your use of, or reliance on, information provided on this website.

Information we collect

We only collect personal information that you choose to give us, or that we reasonably need to respond to you. This includes:

  • Contact and enquiry details — such as your name, email address, phone number, business name, and any message or details you provide when you submit a form, request a sample report, or book a call.
  • Booking information — when you schedule a consultation, the details you enter into our booking calendar (for example, your name, email, and any notes).
  • Technical information — like most websites, our hosting and content providers may automatically record limited technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit, for security and to deliver the site.

We do not ask for, and you should not send us, sensitive information (such as health, financial account, or government identifier details) through this website.

The risk tools

Our interactive tools (for example, “Find your line”) run in your own browser to show you a result. When you reach the tool’s results step — and again if you choose to book a call — the figures involved are sent to us and stored. This includes the revenue, surplus cash, and industry you entered, the worst-loss figure you set, and the results we calculate from them, together with a random session reference so we can understand how the tool is used and follow up on genuine enquiries.

These tool figures are not linked to your name or contact details unless you separately provide them — for example, by booking a call or submitting a form.

How we use your information

We use your personal information to:

  • respond to your enquiry, request, or booking;
  • provide the information, materials, or consultation you have asked for;
  • operate, secure, and improve this website; and
  • meet our legal and record-keeping obligations.

We will only use your details to contact you about your enquiry or matters related to it. We do not send marketing you have not asked for, and we do not sell your personal information.

Who we share it with

We do not disclose your personal information except to trusted service providers who help us run the website and respond to you. These currently include:

  • Google — form submissions and booking details are handled using Google services (such as Google Workspace, Google Sheets, and Google Calendar), and some page fonts are served by Google Fonts.
  • Cloudflare — provides hosting and security for this website.

These providers may store or process information on servers located outside Australia, including in the United States. We take reasonable steps to use reputable providers that handle information securely. We may also disclose information where required or authorised by law.

Cookies and analytics

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Our hosting and embedded services (such as the booking calendar and fonts) may set limited technical cookies needed to function. You can control or block cookies through your browser settings; some embedded features may not work if you do.

Keeping your information secure

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access, and to destroy or de-identify it when it is no longer needed. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Accessing and correcting your information

You can ask us for a copy of the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable time. There is normally no charge for this.

Complaints

If you have a concern about how we have handled your personal information, please contact us first and we will try to resolve it. If you are not satisfied, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au or on 1300 363 992.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be posted on this page, with the “last updated” date shown above.
