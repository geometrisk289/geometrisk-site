Why the conversation is hard to have

Most businesses can't see the size of what they're carrying.

So they fall back on the one number they can see – the premium. It isn't carelessness. Nobody has ever shown them the shape of their own risk. Show them, and price stops being the whole conversation.

Every one of these is a business holding a loss it has never measured. The risk is already theirs. The only question is whether anyone has helped them look at it.

The other half of the job

A policy answers the loss after it lands.

That's what it's built for, and it does it well. Our work sits earlier: the losses a client could make smaller first, and the ones worth understanding before any decision. Working that out is a different job from placing cover. It takes time, and a method. Two things a busy book rarely has to spare, which is exactly where we sit alongside you.

The price conversation
